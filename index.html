Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?
We recommend visiting every 6 months for a routine check-up and professional cleaning. Regular visits help detect problems early and keep your smile in top condition.
What should I do in a dental emergency?
Call our clinic immediately at +91-9428513120. We provide same-day emergency care for severe pain, broken teeth, swelling, or trauma.
Do you provide dental care for children?
Absolutely! We offer gentle, child-friendly pediatric dental care for kids of all ages — making dental visits a positive experience from an early age.
What are my options for replacing missing teeth?
We offer dental implants, bridges, and dentures depending on your needs, bone structure, and budget. Dr. Prachi will guide you to the best solution.
Is root canal treatment painful?
Not at all — with modern techniques and effective local anaesthesia, root canal treatment at our clinic is virtually painless. Most patients are surprised by how comfortable the procedure is.
Does Hariom Dental Clinic offer Invisalign?
Yes! We are trained in Invisalign clear aligner therapy. It's a discreet, comfortable, and effective way to straighten your teeth without traditional metal braces.
What makes Dr. Prachi Patel the right choice?
With 10+ years of experience, specialized training across multiple disciplines, a compassionate approach, and transparent pricing — Dr. Prachi combines expertise with genuine patient care that is hard to find.
